Welcome to a Sales Prediction project!

What's this all about?

Our Sales Prediction project aims to provide accurate and timely predictions to help you make data-driven decisions. The dataset contains data from the Supply Chain domain and it was formed from Fabian Constante, Fernando Silva, António Pereira. You can find more about the dataset here.

Features

We offer a variety of features including data analytics, trend forecasting, and much more.
